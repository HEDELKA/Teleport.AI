Teleport.AI

One question. One answer. One scroll.

❌ Problem

Users waste 10–30 clicks just to find price, delivery, or support. This kills conversions and frustrates users.

✅ Solution

Teleport.AI is an AI assistant embedded in your site. Ask a question, and get teleported directly to the answer.

⚙️ How it works

  • JS widget on your website
  • Site indexing via LangChain / JS parser
  • LLM-based question understanding
  • DOM navigation (scrollIntoView, click)

🌟 Use Cases

  • E-commerce shops
  • Banks and payment systems
  • SaaS platforms & support centers
  • Government and service portals

📈 Why Now

  • Exploding open-source LLM space
  • Chat interfaces becoming the norm
  • Companies crave simplified UX
  • No one built AI navigators yet
